Privacy Policy of Erklärbär

This application uses some personal data from its Users

Audio Data

If you want to use any of the voice functionalities the local recording will be shared with the speech recognition provider you are using. For Android devices that is Google and for IOS devices it is Apple. In special cases your audio data can be processed on your device and is not shared with any third party.

External Sorage

The storage of your device is only used for app internal data and reading any files you want to include in the application. Your included images and videos are stored on your local device.
