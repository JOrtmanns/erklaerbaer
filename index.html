Erklärbär

Store your personal instructions for you or family members

Ever explained something to a famaly member. Maybe a grandparent, or parent. Then you know that after your explanaiton you had to explain the same thing over and over again.

If they could use google or similar tools to search for the explanations they wouldn't need your help in the first place.

With Erklärbär you create your own instructions with images, videos and text. They can be searched by talking to the app.

Be the face of your families and friends instructions.
